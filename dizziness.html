Dizziness Information

Symptoms

  • Feeling lightheaded or faint
  • Loss of balance or unsteadiness
  • Spinning sensation (vertigo)
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Feeling disoriented

Medicines

  • Meclizine (Antivert)
  • Dimenhydrinate (Dramamine)
  • Prochlorperazine (Compazine)
  • Betahistine (Serc)

Prevention

  1. Stay hydrated to prevent dehydration.
  2. Avoid sudden movements or changes in position.
  3. Limit alcohol and caffeine intake.
  4. Get up slowly from sitting or lying positions.
  5. Manage stress through relaxation techniques.
