Stomach Ache Information

Symptoms

  • Abdominal pain or cramping
  • Feeling bloated
  • Gas and flatulence
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea or constipation
  • Lack of appetite

Medicines

  • Antacids (e.g., Tums, Maalox)
  • Proton pump inhibitors (e.g., omeprazole)
  • Laxatives or stool softeners (for constipation)
  • Antidiarrheal medications (e.g., loperamide)

Prevention

  1. Avoid overeating or eating too quickly.
  2. Limit spicy and fatty foods.
  3. Stay hydrated by drinking enough water.
  4. Practice good food hygiene.
  5. Manage stress through relaxation techniques.

Prescriptions

Consult your healthcare provider for appropriate treatments and prescriptions based on your symptoms and health condition.
