Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services do you offer?

We offer a range of services including primary care, preventive care, and specialized treatments tailored to meet your health needs.

2. How can I make an appointment?

You can make an appointment by calling our office or using the online appointment system on our website.

3. What should I bring to my first appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card (if applicable), and any relevant medical records.

4. Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please check with our office for specific details.

5. What if I don't have insurance?

We offer a sliding scale fee based on income for those without insurance. Please contact our office for more information.

6. Are your services confidential?

Yes, all patient information is kept confidential according to HIPAA regulations.

7. How can I access my medical records?

You can request access to your medical records through our patient portal or by contacting our office directly.

8. What are your office hours?

Our office is open Monday to Friday from 8 AM to 5 PM, and Saturday from 9 AM to 1 PM.
