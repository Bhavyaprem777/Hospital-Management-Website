Sore Throat Information

Symptoms

  • Scratchy or painful sensation in the throat
  • Difficult or painful swallowing
  • Red or swollen tonsils
  • Swollen lymph nodes in the neck
  • Fever
  • Hoarse or muffled voice

Medicines

  • Ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin)
  • Acetaminophen (Tylenol)
  • Throat lozenges (e.g., Cepacol)
  • Saltwater gargle

Prevention

  1. Wash hands frequently to prevent infections.
  2. Avoid close contact with individuals who are sick.
  3. Practice good respiratory hygiene (cover mouth/nose when coughing/sneezing).
  4. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of fluids.
  5. Avoid irritants such as smoke and strong odors.

Prescriptions

Consult your healthcare provider for appropriate treatments and prescriptions based on your symptoms and health condition.
