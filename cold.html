Common Cold Information

Symptoms

  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Sore throat
  • Cough
  • Congestion
  • Minor body aches
  • Fatigue
  • Headache
  • Fever (sometimes)

Medicines

  • Antihistamines (e.g., diphenhydramine)
  • Decongestants (e.g., pseudoephedrine)
  • Cough suppressants (e.g., dextromethorphan)
  • Pain relievers (e.g., acetaminophen, ibuprofen)

Prevention

  1. Wash your hands frequently.
  2. Avoid close contact with infected individuals.
  3. Do not touch your face with unwashed hands.
  4. Maintain a healthy immune system through diet and exercise.
  5. Consider getting a flu vaccine.

Prescriptions

Consult your healthcare provider for appropriate treatments and prescriptions based on your symptoms and health condition.
