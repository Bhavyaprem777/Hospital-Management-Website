Back Pain Information

Symptoms

  • Aching or stiffness in the back
  • Pain that radiates down the leg
  • Muscle spasms
  • Difficulty standing up straight
  • Limited range of motion

Medicines

  • Ibuprofen (Advil, Motrin)
  • Acetaminophen (Tylenol)
  • Muscle relaxants
  • Topical pain relievers (e.g., creams, patches)

Prevention

  1. Maintain a healthy weight to reduce strain on your back.
  2. Exercise regularly to strengthen back and core muscles.
  3. Practice good posture when sitting and standing.
  4. Use ergonomic furniture and tools at work.
  5. Avoid heavy lifting and improper bending.

Prescriptions

Consult your healthcare provider for appropriate treatments and prescriptions based on your symptoms and health condition.
