Fatigue Information

Symptoms

  • Constant tiredness or sleepiness
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Muscle weakness
  • Memory problems
  • Increased irritability
  • Headaches
  • Low energy levels

Medicines

  • Stimulants (e.g., caffeine)
  • Modafinil
  • Antidepressants (if fatigue is related to depression)
  • Vitamin and mineral supplements (if deficiencies are present)

Prevention

  1. Maintain a regular sleep schedule.
  2. Engage in regular physical activity.
  3. Eat a balanced diet rich in nutrients.
  4. Manage stress through relaxation techniques.
  5. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.

Prescriptions

Consult your healthcare provider for appropriate treatments and prescriptions based on your symptoms and health condition.
