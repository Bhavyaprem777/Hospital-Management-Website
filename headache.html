Headache Information

Symptoms

  • Pain on one or both sides of the head
  • Throbbing or pulsing sensation
  • Sensitivity to light and sound
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Blurred vision
  • Tightness or pressure across the forehead

Medicines

  • Over-the-counter pain relievers (e.g., ibuprofen, acetaminophen)
  • Triptans (e.g., sumatriptan, rizatriptan)
  • Anti-nausea medications
  • Prescription pain relief medications (consult a doctor)

Prevention

  1. Identify and avoid headache triggers.
  2. Maintain a regular sleep schedule.
  3. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  4. Practice relaxation techniques (e.g., yoga, meditation).
  5. Maintain a balanced diet.

Prescriptions

Consult your healthcare provider for appropriate treatments and prescriptions based on your symptoms and health condition.
