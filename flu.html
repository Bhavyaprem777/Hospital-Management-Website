Flu Information

Symptoms

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Sore throat
  • Runny or stuffy nose
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headaches
  • Fatigue
  • Some people may experience vomiting and diarrhea

Medicines

  • Oseltamivir (Tamiflu)
  • Zanamivir (Relenza)
  • Peramivir (Rapivab)
  • Baloxavir marboxil (Xofluza)

Prevention

  1. Get vaccinated each year.
  2. Practice good hygiene by washing your hands frequently.
  3. Avoid close contact with sick individuals.
  4. Stay home when you are feeling unwell.
  5. Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your elbow when you cough or sneeze.

Prescriptions

Consult your healthcare provider for appropriate prescriptions based on your symptoms and health condition.
